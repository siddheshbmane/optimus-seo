## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Optimus SEO is an AI-agent-powered SEO operations platform that combines:
1. **DataForSEO APIs** (12 categories, 100+ endpoints) for real SEO data
2. **LLM-powered AI Agents** for intelligent analysis and automation
3. **AI/LLM Visibility Tracking** - unique differentiator (no competitor has this)

### Current State
- **41+ routes built** with full interactivity
- **Zero dead clicks** - every button, link, dropdown works
- **Deployed:** https://optimus-seo.vercel.app
- **Tech Stack:** Next.js 15 + Tailwind CSS v4 + shadcn/ui + Zustand + Recharts

### Target State
- **60+ routes** with 10 new major modules
- **Full DataForSEO integration** (mock-first, then real API)
- **15+ AI agents** for autonomous SEO operations
- **Industry-first AI visibility tracking**

### Your Scenario: 10 Clients × 350 Keywords × 3 Locations

| Scenario | DataForSEO/mo | LLM/mo | Total/mo | Per Client |
|----------|---------------|--------|----------|------------|
| Basic | $25 | $0 (Groq Free) | $25 | $2.50 |
| Standard | $139 | $0 | $139 | $13.90 |
| Full Suite | $452 | $0-15 | $467 | $46.70 |
| Enterprise | $1,342 | $50 | $1,392 | $139.20 |

### LLM Strategy
- **Primary:** Groq Free Tier (Llama 3.3 70B) - 30,000 requests/month FREE
- **Fallback:** OpenRouter (Llama 3.3 70B) - FREE
- **Premium:** GPT-4o-mini ($0.15/1M input, $0.60/1M output) - when needed

### Key Decision
**NO AI MODEL TRAINING REQUIRED** - LLMs work via prompting, not fine-tuning.
